[B] 12. Etelka’s Bedroom

- Etelka’s clothes are scattered across the floor, mixed with Gutbag’s extensive collection of finger bones. Stuck on a bedpost is a severed Dwarven head. Hanging on the wall is a picture of Etelka: if the adventurers have seen her on their journey to Grissenwald, they will not fail to recognise her.

A wooden chest lies against the west wall, which can be opened with a

[B] Hard (-20) Pick Lock

Test, SL5. Gutbag keeps the key on a chain around his neck. Inside the chest are 28 Gold Crowns, 85 Shillings, 105 Brass Pennies and Etelka’s silverware

— 12 silver plates (1 GCs each), 12 knife, fork & spoon sets (2 GCs each), and one salt and pepper set (2 GCs). Loose among the coins are 18 silver rings (worth 2d10 x 10 silver shillings in total), 37 earrings (worth 4D10 x 10 silver shillings in total), and an assortment of half-empty perfume bottles.
