[H2] The Dwarfs of Khazid Slumbol

[B] Durak Dimholt

Durak is a dour and determined character who has stuck with his clan through thick and thin, despite wanting to leave after the mine was sold. He was ambushed by a Goblin patrol while out hunting. If he is saved Durak will be very grateful, and after he has healed fully he will be interested in joining the adventurers and putting Khazid Slumbol behind him. However, if the party includes one or more Elves, they must each make a

[B] Hard (-20) Charm

Test or Durak will refuse to accompany them.
